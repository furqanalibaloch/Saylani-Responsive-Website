Saylani Welfare International Trust is one of the leading NGOs in Pakistan, established in May 1999 by famous spiritual and religious scholar Moulana Muhammad Bashir Farooqui. Saylani Welfare International Trust was built on the fundamentals of breaking the cycle of poverty, alleviating the financial troubles of the poor, giving people a chance to live a dignified life and spreading happiness.

The organization believes in lighting up the lives of underprivileged people in different countries of the world. We endeavor to provide the best quality services in areas including food, education, medical and social welfare free of cost to people living in the dark. With over 63 different sectors, we feed thousands of hungry people each day, hundreds more are given the hope of life through medical health care, many are educated to become leaders of tomorrow and several are given the opportunity to stand on their feet financially.

Today, we are proud to say that our physical presence extends throughout Pakistan with a vast network of 150 branches operating in major cities including Karachi, Thatta, Hyderabad, Tando M Khan, Quetta, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Samundri, Chiniot, and Swabi.

Apart from Pakistan, we have overseas offices in the UK. Our nationwide branches are operating under the guidance of a team of over 3,500 working professionals who help almost 150,000 people on a daily basis in areas such as education, health care, food, social welfare, the supply of clean drinking water and disaster aid to the deserving and distressed.
